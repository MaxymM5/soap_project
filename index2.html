About us
How it started

Beneath the Vinnytsia sun, four of us decided to add a touch of fragrance to life and opened several small PureLux stores – our little corners of luxurious soap. The initial months were modest: scents mingled with the urban air, and customers approached us cautiously.

While there was no immediate success, each customer who chose our soap brought a bit of warmth to our stalls. We patiently cultivated our customer base and crafted each soap with care. Despite modest profits, we believed that time would bring a genuine scent of success to our PureLux shops.
